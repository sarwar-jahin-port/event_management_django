Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about EventPro.

How do I create an event?

Simply sign up for an EventPro account, get organizer access from an admin, and use our intuitive event creation form to set up your event with all the details.

Is EventPro free to use?

Yes! EventPro is completely free for both event organizers and participants. Create events, RSVP, and manage your attendance at no cost.

How do I RSVP to an event?

Browse our events page, find an event you're interested in, and click "View Details." You'll need to sign in to RSVP, then simply click the "Attend" button.

Can I cancel my RSVP?

Absolutely! You can cancel your RSVP anytime by visiting the event page and clicking the "Cancel Attendance" button. We understand plans change.

How do I become an organizer?

Contact our admin team through this contact form or email us directly. We'll review your request and grant organizer permissions so you can start creating events.

Do you offer event analytics?

Yes! Organizers get access to real-time RSVP tracking, participant lists, and basic analytics to help make their events successful.
